# Fridge Feast

Fridge Feast is a smart recipe generator that helps you discover new meals you can create with the ingredients you already have in your kitchen.

## How it works

Simply add the ingredients you have on hand, and the app's AI will generate a list of creative recipe suggestions, complete with instructions and ingredient lists. It's the perfect way to reduce food waste and find inspiration for your next meal!

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)
- **Language:** [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)
- **AI:** [Genkit](https://firebase.google.com/docs/genkit) with Google's Gemini model
- **UI:** [React](https://react.dev/), [ShadCN](https://ui.shadcn.com/), and [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)

This project was built in Firebase Studio. To get started exploring the code, take a look at `src/app/page.tsx`.
